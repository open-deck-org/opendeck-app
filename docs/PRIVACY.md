# Privacy Policy

**OpenDeck**

_Last updated: 23 June 2026_

OpenDeck is designed to collect nothing. This policy explains, plainly, what
that means.

## Summary

- OpenDeck **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.
- It makes **no network requests** — there are no servers, accounts, analytics,
  ads, or trackers.
- Everything you open or import stays **on your device**.

## What the app stores, and where

OpenDeck is a viewer for self-contained presentation documents ("decks"). When
you import or open a deck, the app saves it **locally on your device** so it
appears in your library:

- On iPhone, iPad, and Mac: in the app's own private storage area, managed by
  the operating system.
- In the web/PWA version: in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB) on your
  own device.

This content never leaves your device through OpenDeck. The app has no backend,
so there is nowhere for it to be sent. Deleting a deck from the library removes
it from local storage; deleting the app removes all of it.

## Information we do **not** collect

We do not collect or process any of the following: name, email, contacts,
location, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, usage analytics, crash
telemetry, cookies, or the contents of your decks. We do not build profiles and
do not sell or share data, because we have none.

## No third parties

OpenDeck bundles its fonts and icons inside the app and does not load anything
from third-party servers or content-delivery networks. It includes **no
third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs**.

> Note: a deck is a self-contained HTML document that runs in an isolated,
> sandboxed environment within the app. OpenDeck itself initiates no network
> activity. The sandbox is configured to keep deck content contained and unable
> to reach the rest of the app.

## Children's privacy

OpenDeck is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children. Because no personal information is ever gathered, the app does not
knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in
your jurisdiction).

## Your rights

Since OpenDeck holds no personal data about you on any server, there is nothing
for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full
control of the decks stored locally on your device and can remove them at any
time.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address
